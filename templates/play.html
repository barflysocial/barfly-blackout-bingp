❓ How To Play / ⚡ Power Guide

Mark only the current called letter-number. The board has 25 lines with 3 numbers per line. Clear a full line to remove it. Every 4 cleared lines earns a random power after at least 10 calls. A Full Clear happens when all lines are gone.

🍀 Lucky Spot: free spot. 👀 Number Peek: see next number. 🔄 Second Chance: blocks attack. ❄️ Freeze: target misses current/next call. 🚧 Row Block: blocks a line. 🃏 Card Shuffle: shuffles uncalled numbers. 💥 Shield Breaker: removes protection. 🪞 Mirror Attack: reflects an attack. 🔄 Power Swap: swaps powers.
