❓ How To Play / ⚡ Power Guide

Mark only the current called number. Horizontal rows earn Bingo credit. Every 4 rows on a card earns 1 random power. Blackouts determine ranking first, then timestamp and points.

🍀 Lucky Spot: free spot. 👀 Number Peek: see next number. 🔄 Second Chance: blocks attack. ❄️ Freeze: target misses current/next call. 🚧 Row Block: blocks a row. 🃏 Card Shuffle: shuffles uncalled numbers. 💥 Shield Breaker: removes protection. 🪞 Mirror Attack: reflects an attack. 🔄 Power Swap: swaps powers.
