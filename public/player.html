How to Play

  • Each card is a 5x5 BINGO card.
  • There are no free spaces.
  • When a number is called, it turns orange.
  • Tap an orange number to select it. Selected numbers turn green.
  • Numbers are never auto-selected.
  • Blackout means all 25 spaces on one card are green.

Leaderboard

The leaderboard shows each player's best card as a progress bar like 18/25.

When a player wins, the system records 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place with server time rounded to the nearest tenth of a second.
