How to Play

  • 5x5 BINGO cards. No free spaces.
  • Called numbers turn orange.
  • Tap orange numbers to select them. Selected numbers turn green.
  • Numbers are never auto-selected.
  • Blackout means all 25 spaces on one card are green.

Winners

1st, 2nd, and 3rd place are detected automatically and timestamped to the nearest tenth of a second.

After 3rd place, the board clears after 1 minute while players stay checked in.
